The RISC-V Summit Europe will be a unique opportunity for members of the ecosystem to engage with the broader community. Becoming a sponsor will enable your organisation to connect with attendees from industry, government, research and academia, developing applications across a range of markets.

There are three levels of sponsorship package, with benefits that include speaking opportunities, promotional opportunities, exhibition space and conference passes. A la carte opportunities are also available

Diamond Sponsorship - 16.5K Euros SOLD OUT

  • Speaking slot: 15-minute keynote or 20 minutes in a track session (Automotive, HPC, Tools, Security AI)
  • Largest booth space 3x2 (Sponsor to either bring their own custom stand or select pre-fabbed frame)
  • 8 full-conference passes (total for staff and customers)
  • Access to meeting room for booking private meetings
  • 2-minute video with RISC-V CEO Calista Redmond (priority in the event playlist)
  • 15-minute demo theater presentation (1) - recorded (priority in the demo playlist)
  • Talk promoted in pre-show email
  • Demo promoted in pre-show email
  • Logo on event giveaway
  • Logo on slide/signage
  • 15 percent discount for 10 additional full-conference passes

Gold Sponsorship - 8.5K Euros

  • 4 full-conference passes (Total for staff and customers)
  • Tabletop, 2 chairs
  • 10-minute demo theater presentation (1) - recorded
  • Demo promoted in pre-show email
  • Logo on slide/signage
  • Break co-sponsorship
  • 15 percent discount for 5 additional full-conference passes

Silver Sponsorship: 2 Options - 2.5K Euros

  • Option 1 (Exhibition Sponsorship)
    • 1 tabletop, 2 chairs
    • 1 full-conference pass
    • Logo on slide; signage
  • Option 2 (Silver Sponsorship - Community Support)
    • 2 full-conference passes
    • Logo on slide/signage

Other options

  • Lanyards Sponsorship (1) – 3000 Euros
  • Tuesday Community Reception Sponsorship (2) – 5000 Euros each
  • Student/DEI Sponsors – Provide financial support/scholarship to need-demonstrated students or under-represented individuals to attend the event.
    • Sponsor 10 qualified individuals (3000 Euros)
    • Sponsor 5 qualified individuals (1750 Euros)

Sponsorships will be taken on a first-come, first-served basis. Priority is based on when completed sponsor form is received by Technical Secretariat (BCO).

Sponsorship opportunities are available now!

Please contact Andrew Moore (europe-summit-marketing@riscv.org) for questions.
