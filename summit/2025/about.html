The Summit

The two previous editions of the RISC-V Summit Europe were:

  • 24-28 June 2024, Munich.
  • 5-9 June 2023, Barcelona.

The RISC-V Summit Europe Series started as a merger of three previous European events series, joined by RISC-V International:

  • Red-RISC-V in Spain: http://red-riscv.org
  • RISC-V Week in France: https://open-src-soc.org/2022-05
  • Workshop on RISC-V activities in Germany: https://www.edacentrum.de/en/risc-v
