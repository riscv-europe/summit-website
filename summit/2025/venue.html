Venue

RISC-V Summit Europe 2025 will take place at La Cité des Science et de l’Industrie in Paris.

La Cité des Sciences et de l’Industrie is located 30 avenue Corentin-Cariou, and can be reached by metro M7 and tramway T3b.

Accomodation

There are plenty of accomodation options in Paris.

Several hotels are close to the venue (in order of proximity):

  • ReMIX hotel.
  • Hilton Garden Inn Paris La Villette.
