Venue

RISC-V Summit Europe 2026 will take place at Palazzo dei Congressi in Bologna.

The Palazzo dei Congressi is located Piazza della Costituzione 4/A, 40128 Bologna, and can be reached by the bus lines 28, 35, 38 & 39 from the city center.

Accomodation

There are plenty of accomodation options in Bologna.

You can find more information for accomodation on the dedicated web page of Bologna Welcome.

Local print shop

If you do not want to carry your poster all the way to Bologna…

If you want to print your poster on-site instead of bringing it from home to Bologna, here is a list of print shops in downtdown Bologna. Do not hesitate to contact them directly:

  • Copisteria Belle Arti.
  • Centro Stampa Zamboni.
  • University Copy.
  • Copisteria Asterisco.
  • Emergenza Tesi.

Please note that posters must be printed in A0 size, and in portrait mode.
